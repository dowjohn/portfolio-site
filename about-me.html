About Me

The Longest Roads Lead to the Richest Rewards


My life story is a winding road, full of bumps, backtracking, and rich tales of success and failure. Through all of my journeys though, my values have been shaped into the core belief that more and varied experience improves a person mentally and emotionally.

I spent several years in a Drum and Bugle Corps playing contra. I have tinkered with computers, building from parts and modifying existing ones. I have traveled to nearly every state gaining knowledge of the differences between cultures. I spent years studying as a classically trained bassoonist.

At last I find myself on a career path that I find fulfilling in every respect. One that pushes my mind to learn new things every day. Lifelong learning is critical to my profession and leads to adaptibility which is mandatory for success.

Lastly, I bring professionalism and an efficient work ethic to the workplace, wherever that may and whatever the conditions, every day.
